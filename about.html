About Us

Founded by pickleball players, for pickleball players

Our Story

Dink Gear Shop started on a court in Los Angeles with a simple frustration: great pickleball gear was hard to find and even harder to trust. We spent hours researching paddles, testing balls, and returning shoes that just didn't hold up.

So we decided to build the store we always wished existed — one that curates only the best equipment, explains what actually matters, and ships fast. Whether you're picking up a paddle for the first time or you've been dinking since before it was cool, we've got gear for your game.
