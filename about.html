Our Story

Pickleball is one of the fastest growing sports in the world — and we're obsessed with it.

It started the way most good things do: a group of friends on a court, completely hooked after their first game. Pickleball is fast, fun, competitive, and social all at once. It doesn't matter if you're 20 or 60 — once you start playing, you can't stop.

After a while we wanted to wear our love for the game. Custom shirts, hats, shoes — something that said "I play pickleball and I'm proud of it." But finding quality, actually cool pickleball apparel? Way harder than it should be.

So we made our own. Dink Gear Shop is where pickleball culture meets custom style. We design pieces you'll actually want to wear — on the court and off it.
